About Us

Huda Islamic Da'wa Students' Association ~ Students' union of Sirajul Huda Hifz Da'wa - Nadapuram
HIDA is the student body of the great institution of Sirajul Huda Hifz Da'wa located in the historic Nadapuram. Such an organization was formed to nurture the artistic talents of the students and to inspire them in their academic pursuits.
